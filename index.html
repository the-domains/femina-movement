What is FEMINA Movement?

It is about Feeling, Empowering, Inspiring, Nourishing, and Aligning our Mind, Body and Spirits.

It is about how our individual attitudes and actions towards ourselves and one another can lead to either self-love, or continued self-consciousness – it’s our choice!
